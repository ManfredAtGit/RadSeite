Manfred's Cycling Site

Cycling is cool!


Cycling has become a real passion of mine. At first it merely served as a substitution for other typs of sport I couldn't carry out anymore due to a severe arthrosis in my angle joints. Nowadays it has evolved to a lifestyle embracing the sportive rationale as well as the fine art of randonneuring and bike touring.

Cycling also serves as the central content theme in developing this (experimental) website using techniques like bootstrap, jQuery, d3js and more.
